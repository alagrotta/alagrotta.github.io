Ever wonder how many miles you have left when your gas light comes on?

Search no further!

Follow these simple 3 steps below to learn how to easily calculate it!

Step 1:

The next time you're at a gas station, fill up a full tank of gas, and then RESET your trip meter. This can usually be done by holding down the little peg next to the display.

Check your car's owners manual for more information on resetting the trip meter.

Step 2:

When your gas light comes on, stop at the next gas station and fill up your tank again.

Now divide the # of miles recorded on your trip meter by the # of gallons you just filled up (found on the receipt).
